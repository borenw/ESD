3. Reading notes — what the asymmetry does during a zap

Fingers 1,2 are the new green pair. With the gout tap at the left, the M1 ladder steers ~2× the current of pair 5,6 into pair 1,2 (46% vs 23%). The nearest pair to the tap point runs hottest and filaments first — the layout reproduces exactly the mixed-ballast lean case (0 / 2 / 4 Ω on top of ~4 Ω pair RPO ≈ 4/6/8 Ω totals → ~60/40-style lean spread over three rungs).
Fixes that flatten the ladder: center-tap the gout bus between D₃₄ (drops worst-case to 2 Ω), widen the bus so each hop <0.5 Ω, or fishbone-tap all three drain stubs to a wide trunk. Alternatively exploit it: intentionally shorten RPO on pairs 3–6 by the metal delta so Σ R matches 8 Ω on all rungs.
